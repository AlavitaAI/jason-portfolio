📖 Scoring Rules
Detailed breakdown of the World Cup 2026 Prediction Pool points system.

📈 Points Breakdown

Points are awarded for correct predictions across the group stage and the knockout tournament. Each point is scaled by the team's Tier Multiplier.

Prediction Stage Base Points Scoring Type
Group Stage: Advancement 10 pts Per correct team predicted to finish 1st or 2nd and advance.
Group Winner Bonus +5 pts Extra bonus if you correctly predict the exact 1st place team.
Group Runner-Up Bonus +2 pts Extra bonus if you correctly predict the exact 2nd place team.
3rd Place Qualifiers 10 pts Per correct team predicted to be in the top 8 advancing 3rd-place teams (from your ranked top 8).
Round of 32 Match Win 5 pts For each correct winner chosen to advance to the Round of 16.
Round of 16 Match Win 10 pts For each correct winner chosen to advance to the Quarterfinals.
Quarterfinal Match Win 20 pts For each correct winner chosen to advance to the Semifinals.
Semifinal Match Win 40 pts For each correct winner chosen to advance to the Final.
Third-Place Match Win 40 pts For correctly predicting the winner of the 3rd-place playoff match.
Final Match Win 80 pts For correctly predicting the winner of the Final match.
Tournament Champion 150 pts Extra bonus for correctly predicting the overall World Cup winner (added to Final win points).

🧮 Calculation Examples

Example 1: Group Stage (USA - Tier 3, ×1.7)
If you predict USA to finish 1st in Group D, and they actually finish 1st:
• Correct Advancement: 10 pts × 1.7 = 17 pts
• Group Winner Bonus: 5 pts × 1.7 = 8.5 pts (rounded)
• Total: 25.5 pts (final score rounded to 26 pts).
Example 2: Knockout Stages (Haiti - Tier 6, ×4.0)
If you predict Haiti to advance in the Round of 32, and they pull off an upset:
• Round of 32 Win: 5 pts × 4.0 = 20 pts!
• Comparison: France (Tier 1) winning their Round of 32 match yields only 5 pts (5 × 1.0).
